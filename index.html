Request Account Deletion

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data for TaskGame.

To request account deletion, please email vdzhos.apps@gmail.com with the subject Delete Account.

We will process your request within 30 days.

What will be deleted

Upon confirming your request, we will delete your account and associated personal data that we control.

Identity verification

We may ask you to verify your identity or ownership of the account to prevent unauthorized deletion requests.

Need help?

If you have questions before submitting a request, contact us at vdzhos.apps@gmail.com.
